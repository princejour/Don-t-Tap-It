App Name:
Don’t Tap It!

Short Description:
A silly premium one-tap game where the button fights back.

Full Description:
Don’t Tap It! is a fast, funny, premium-looking one-tap game where your only mission is simple: tap the button before it escapes. Sounds easy? It is not. The button moves, shrinks, tricks you with fake buttons, drops bombs, and throws sarcastic messages at you.

Unlock skins, beat your best score, complete daily missions, and prove that your finger is faster than the most dramatic button on your phone.

Features:
- Fast one-tap gameplay
- Premium neon visual style
- Funny sarcastic messages
- Unlockable button skins
- Daily missions
- Offline gameplay
- No account required
- Lightweight and mobile-friendly
